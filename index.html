About Me

I use my background in equity, social justice, and intersectionality and my understanding of inequitable structures and power relations to build robust relationships across difference among colleagues and community stakeholders. This unique knowledge lends itself to UX research and design for equity and accessibility. I am a manager in an academic diversity unit with nine years of experience in marketing and communication who seeks a position in at an organization with which I share common values. I maintain a focus on the overall goals and mission of my workplace while always thinking critically to encourage growth.

Resume

Graphic Designer

After nearly 20 years of being a purely self-trained graphic designer for social change organizations I worked for, in 2021, I took my first formal design class. I create solutions-oriented digital and print designs to meet goals. I collaborate with teams to consistently produce results. Communication and inviting critique are my strengths. I specialize in designing for social media, annual reports, posters, flyers, and infographics, and resumes.

UX Researcher and Designer

My commitment to universal access, problem solving, and critical thinking shine through in my UX research and design. I am driven by creativity and empathy. I put all of my research skills to use toward designing UX that works for everyone. Collaboration, teamwork, and user-first thinking are my greatest strengths in UX work.
